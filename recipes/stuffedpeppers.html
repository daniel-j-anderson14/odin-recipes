Stuffed Peppers

Description

Stuffed peppers filled with ground beef and rice are easy to make and delicious. You will love this recipe.

These stuffed peppers will take about an hour to bake and must be fully cooked because they contain ground beef. You will be able to tell when they are done when the peppers are tender.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1/2 cup uncooked long grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 6 medium bell peppers
  • 16 oz tomato sauce, divided
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 320 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Bring water and rice to a boil in a covered saucepan. Reduce heat to low and simmer unitl rice is tender
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add ground beef and cook until evenly browned.
  4. Hollow out bell peppers by removing and discarding tops, seeds, and membranes. Slice bottoms if required to allow peppers to stand straight up.
  5. Arrange peppers in a baking dish with hollowed side facing up
  6. Prepare filling by mixing cooked beef and rice with 1/2 of the tomato sauce, the Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Spoon into hollowed out peppers.
  7. Mix remaining tomato sauc and Italian seasoning and pour over peppers
  8. Bake in preheated oven, basting with sauce every 15 minutes until peppers are tender.
